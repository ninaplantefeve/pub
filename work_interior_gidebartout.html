GIDE BAR TOUT

An international project managed with 4 people,

Our group has the goal to make the lunch room from the campus in Sint-Katelijne-Waver more attractive by adding a function. We would like to turn the lunch room into a market, so it will no longer be a place where you can just eat, but you can shop as well.

We made several elements, hanging from the ceiling on steel rods, on which you can place your items. In order to make these elements functional, they had to be general-purposed.

It doesn’t matter what you want to sell or exhibit, everything is possible due to the universal design. Furthermore, the hanging elements attract your attention when you enter the new lunch room.

For these elements, we would use plywood because of its rewarding qualities. It has the looks and the strength. And to expand the capacity of the elements, we have provided some detachable boxes. This makes Bar Tout an elegant, yet notable design.

So if you are a student, a teacher or just someone who wants to let people know your product: Bar Tout is the perfect spot spot to promote your goods or items.
