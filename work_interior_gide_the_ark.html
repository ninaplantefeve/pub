GIDE THE ARK

An international project managed with 6 people, Security and shelter, the most basic human needs. With the Ark project we are looking to provide a place to flee to, in case of a natural disaster. The objective of our project is to build a network of refugee containers.

They provide a recognisable construction to look for relatives and friends, seek first aid, distraction for children and a place to rest.

Due to a lack of space and a shortage of buildings the construction needs to find a purpose when not needed for emergencies. It can become a location for various events. The sides can be opened in order to increase space and make the site more inviting. In case of no disaster occurring, the containers turn into a social meeting point, open for communal events and hangouts.
